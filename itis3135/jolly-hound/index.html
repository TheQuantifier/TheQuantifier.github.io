Jolly Hound

Jolly Hound is a whimsical micro-kiosk that turns short messages into tiny “howl capsules.” Each capsule includes a bone-shaped keepsake with a QR linking to your custom audio howl-greeting, a playful “wag” animation, and an optional scratch-n-sniff sticker to seal the moment.

Think of us as joy on demand, packaged like a pup’s best day: bright, bouncy, and a little ridiculous—in the best way. Perfect for birthdays, congrats, “miss you,” apology howls, and just-because barks.

What we “sell”

  • HowlGrams

    Personalized audio howls (you or us!) delivered by QR in a bone capsule.

  • WagTags

    Reusable NFC/QR tags for lockers, gift bags, and keychains—swap in new howls anytime.

  • SniffStickers

    Scratch-n-sniff seals (vanilla biscuit, fresh grass, rain-day walk).

  • Pup-Post

    Mini gift wrap + ribbon sets sized for our capsules.
