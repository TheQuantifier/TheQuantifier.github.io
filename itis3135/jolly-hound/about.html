Jolly Hound

Mascot: Jolly Hound (adjective shares your first initial “J”; animal shares your last initial “H”).

What we do: We package tiny celebrations called HowlGrams. You upload or record a short message; we mix in a cheerful howl and a looped “tail-wag” animation, then tuck it all behind a QR in a bone-shaped capsule. Add SniffStickers to make the unseal moment smell like victory (or vanilla biscuits), and finish with Pup-Post wrap. For repeat senders, WagTags let you point a tag at any new message later—perfect for locker doors and gift bags.

Why it fits the theme: Hounds howl and fetch joy. The brand stays light, pun-friendly, and visual—so the “Jolly Hound” identity can show up on icons, packaging, and micro-copy across all pages.

"Give them something to howl about!"
